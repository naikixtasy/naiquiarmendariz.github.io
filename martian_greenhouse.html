Martian Greenhouse Project

STEM Mentorship Initiative in Sustainable Space Agriculture

About the Project

The Martian Greenhouse Initiative is a NASA-led research mentorship program focused on designing food systems for future space missions. As part of a multidisciplinary team, I contributed to the development of an enclosed aeroponic plant habitat for Mars-like environments.

What I Contributed

  • Designed modular 3D-printable platforms and removable plant racks
  • Integrated lighting systems to simulate natural sunlight using LED arrays
  • Implemented aeroponic misting and water filtration concepts for soil-free growth
  • Participated in NASA-led collaborative reviews and presentations
  • Documented design decisions and research highlights for outreach

Why It Matters

Food sustainability is a critical challenge for long-duration space exploration. This project models real-world limitations astronauts may face on Mars or the Moon, emphasizing compact, self-contained systems that conserve water and energy while promoting plant health.
