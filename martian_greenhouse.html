Martian Greenhouse Project

Exploring Sustainable Life on Mars

About the Project

This project is part of the Martian Greenhouse Initiative, where multidisciplinary student teams explore sustainable food systems for future space habitats. We are building a portable, self-sustaining, aeroponic plant growth chamber designed to operate in Martian-like conditions.
