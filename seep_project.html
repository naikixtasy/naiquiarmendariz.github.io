SSEP Research Project

This page showcases my participation in the Student Spaceflight Experiments Program (SSEP), where our team investigated the use of cactus mucilage as a natural water purification agent in microgravity. The experiment was selected for flight aboard the International Space Station (ISS) and later presented at the Smithsonian National Air and Space Museum.

Presentation Highlight

Team Recognition

Selected by the SSEP review board and celebrated at the Smithsonian National Air and Space Museum, our research stood among a select group of flown experiments. This experience reinforced the value of student-led STEM exploration and mentorship.
